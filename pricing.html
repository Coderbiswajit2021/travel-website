Terms & Conditions

  1. Compliance with Driving Rules: Guests are kindly requested not to compel the Driver to exceed RTO or local driving regulations.
  2. Reading Start and End: KM and Time readings will commence and conclude at our garage. Please verify these readings before starting your trip.
  3. Advance Payment: Please provide the driver with cash advance at the beginning of the trip to cover fuel and other expenses.
  4. Driver Working Hours: The driver’s daily working hours are limited to 10 hours. Any extra hours will incur a charge of Rs 100/hr over the regular fare.
  5. Night Stay: In case of over night stay, Rs 600/Night will apply over the normal fare.
  6. Toll Gate and Parking: Guests are responsible for toll gate and parking fees.
  7. AC Usage: The vehicle’s AC will be kept ON during running time only .
  8. Change of Destination: Guests should seek prior approval from Colorscab authority before changing the travel destination.
  9. Liability: Nilachakra tour and travel is not liable for theft, loss, accidents or breakdowns.
  10. Force Majeure: Nilachakra tour and travel reserves the right to cancel a cab in cases of Natural calamities, Public strikes, Government requisitions or Breakdowns.
  11. Payment: All payment should be as per payment schedule.
  12. Cancellation policy: Full booking amount will be refunded if trip will cancel prior to 48 hours of trip time by traveller. 

Booking will be accepted subject to available of cab till you agree with our terms and payment schedule. For booking, we need Traveller Name, Mobile Number, Complete Travel plan and Booking Amount. We will send conformation message from our end after booking. Cab and Driver details will be shared prior to 12 hours of trip time.
