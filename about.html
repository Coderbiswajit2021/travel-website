about us

Welcome to Nilachakra Tour and Travel, where we take pride in presenting visitors with a unique and unforgettable journey through the enchanting state of Odisha, starting from the vibrant temple city of Bhubaneswar. As the leading taxi service in the region, we are committed to providing reliable and affordable transportation options to suit your needs.

At Nilachakra Tour and Travel, our primary focus is on delivering the best service possible, all while maintaining competitive pricing. We understand the importance of a comfortable ride, and we strive to ensure that you have a wonderful and pleasant experience throughout your journey with us. Your satisfaction is our top priority and we take joy in seeing you happy and content.
